COMPLETING SQUARES

Completing the Squares metthod is used when the method of factor (factorization method) fail i.e. when there are no factors or it is used for solving quadratic equations which do not factorize.
Steps:

  1. Divide all the terms by the coefficient of x2
  2. Move the number term(i. the constant term) to the right side of the equation
  3. Complete the squares on the left side of the equation and balance this by adding the same value to the right side of the equation
  4. Take the square root on both sides of the equation
  5. Subtract the number remains on the left side of the equation to find x
