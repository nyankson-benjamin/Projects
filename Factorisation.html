FACTORIZATION METHOD

Factorization is the process of reducing the bracket of a quadratic equation, instead of expanding the bracket and converting the equation to a product of factors which cannot be reduced further

Steps for factorizing quadratic equations

  1. Put the equation in the form ax2 + bx + c =0 by sending all terms to the left hand side of the equation.
  2. Factorize the expression on the left hand side of the equation.
  3. Use the fact that if a and b are real numbers then ab = 0, which also means that either a = 0 or b = 0
